Wrangler Dash

Wrangler Dash is a dashboard I designed to work with my wranglers systems to alert me to issues before they become critical and risk the heath and functionality of the vehicle. It uses a Raspberry Pi as the central computer which is connected to the OBD port using a OBD to USB Connector to receive the information, Soon I hope to have the dashboard send text message to my phone with any warnings that it receives like excessive gas usage, engine startup, low battery, the possibilities are endless.

The current demo is a prototype and is still in development.
Once finished a mock dashboard with dynamic variables will be available.
