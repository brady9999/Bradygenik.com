Apollo

Apollo is a musician and lyricist’s best friend, Users can write or paste their lyrics into the editor, choose the structure, mood, and genre of their track, and get detailed, line‑by‑line feedback from the integrated AI assistant to help polish their writing. Once they’re happy with the final version, they can generate a full song using the same assistant, complete with vocals and instrumentals.

The current demo is a prototype and is still in development.
The previous version of Apollo is available for use at apollo.bradygenik.com
